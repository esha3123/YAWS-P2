Objective

Objective of the Placement Cell The Placement Cell aims to provide students with excellent career opportunities by facilitating campus recruitment, industry interaction, skill development, internships, and career guidance. It strives to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, ensuring students are well-prepared to become competent professionals and responsible citizens. The Cell is also committed to enhancing students’ communication, technical, and leadership skills through training programs, workshops, seminars, mock interviews, and aptitude development sessions. It encourages entrepreneurship, innovation, and continuous learning while building strong partnerships with reputed organizations and industries. The Placement Cell works dedicatedly to support students in achieving their career goals and contributing effectively to society and the nation.
