Student Code of Conduct

Student Code of Conduct Rules
  • Students should take care of college property and help in keeping the college campus neat and clean.
  • Any damage to the college property shall be paid by the student.
  • Minimum water utilization by the students is expected in the campus.
  • For minimum energy consumption all electricity points must be switched off when not in use.
  • Photography in the campus is prohibited unless prior permission of the principal must be obtained.
  • Smoking, chewing Gutka, Tobacco, chewing gums is strictly prohibited in the college campus, including the canteen.
  • Any student found violating these regulations shall be dealt with severely.
  • Use of Mobile phones, iPods, earphones, Mp3 player, headphones etc. in the college premises is strictly prohibited.
  • If the student is found using any of the above mentioned electronic equipment in the college premises, the same (in case of mobile phone, with SIM card, Memory card) will be confiscated and the student will be punished for non adherence of code of conduct.
  • Use of two/four wheelers by student is prohibited in the campus.
  • The management and college authorities are not responsible for any loss or damage of the vehicles.
  • Student found indulging in unfair means in examination will be dealt with strictness as per the university rules and regulations.
  • Students must maintain regular attendance and submit assignments on time.
  • Respect towards teachers and fellow students is mandatory.
  • The students should follow a proper dress code.
  • Short, off shoulders, sleeveless, deep cut dresses should be strictly avoided by girl students.
  • Three fourth length or shorts should be avoided by boy students.
  • College timing is from 12:40 PM to 5:20 PM.
  • Students must be punctual and attend all classes regularly.
⚠️ Important Notice

All students are expected to read and follow the complete code of conduct. Violation of any rule may result in disciplinary action. For detailed guidelines, please refer to the student handbook provided during admission.
